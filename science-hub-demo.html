Day-to-day safety nets

Find anything. Undo almost anything.

Global search

One search box in the top bar finds invoices, customers, products, vendors and bills as you type — no need to know which section it lives in.

Recycle bin, 30 days

Deleted a customer or invoice by mistake? It sits in the bin for 30 days with a reason why it can or can't yet be permanently removed, then restores in one click.

Roles: Admin & Staff

Staff run day-to-day billing and purchasing. Admin-only areas — settings, user management, bulk bin actions — stay locked to owners/managers.

Under the hood, briefly

What it's built on — and why that's a safe choice

Runs in the browser
No software to install on any computer — open the link, log in, and it works on desktop or laptop like any website.
Database
PostgreSQL (via Neon), a mainstream, widely-used database engine — the same category banks and fintechs run on. Automatic backups.
Hosting
Vercel — the same platform behind a large share of production web apps; scales automatically, no server to maintain.
Security
Password-based login with role checks on every action; Gmail app-password and bank account number are encrypted in the database, not stored as plain text.
PDFs & email
Invoices render to PDF in the browser and send via the business's own Gmail account, so customers see a familiar sender.

Getting started

Setup — a one-time, 15-minute job

Deploy the app

Publish the code to Vercel and connect it to a Neon database — a one-time technical step, done once by whoever maintains it.

Create the first admin account

The very first login seeds the owner/admin user. After that, self-signup is disabled — every other account is created by an admin.

Fill in Business Settings

Company name, GSTIN, PAN, address, bank details for invoices, and the Gmail account invoices will be sent from.

Add products, vendors, opening stock

Bring in the existing catalog and current stock counts once — everything after that updates itself.

Add staff accounts

Create a login per staff member with the right role, so every action in the system is attributed to a real person.

Daily use · a sale

From order to payment, in four steps

Pick or add the customer

Search existing customers, or create one inline while raising the invoice — no separate screen needed.

Add line items

Products pull in their price, HSN and GST rate automatically; apply a discount if needed.

Generate & send

Download the PDF or email it straight to the customer from the invoice screen — stock is deducted the moment it's created.

Record the payment

Log full or partial payment as it comes in; the invoice status updates itself to Unpaid, Partial or Paid.

11 / 15
Daily use · a purchase

Stock arrives, the ledger updates itself

Open a new purchase bill

Choose the vendor, enter the line items from their bill, attach a photo or PDF of the physical bill.

Confirm the totals

The system recalculates GST and total independently — a safeguard against a typo on either side.

Save

Stock increases immediately and a matching entry lands in the stock ledger — no extra "receive stock" step.

Pay the vendor

Record the payment against the bill, in full or in parts, whenever it's actually made.
